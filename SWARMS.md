## How I Use These

When you give me a task, I'll:

1. **Assess complexity** — Is it a single-domain or multi-domain task?
2. **Select swarm(s)** — Use the matching table above
3. **Deploy agents** — Spawn sub-agents as needed
4. **Coordinate** — Manage handoffs and parallel work
5. **Deliver** — Present consolidated results

For simple tasks, I work directly. For complex tasks, I activate the appropriate swarm(s).
